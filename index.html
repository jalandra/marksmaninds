Welcome to Marksman Industries

Marksman Industries is a leading manufacturer of high-quality pipe bending tools, SPMs, gauges, jigs, fixtures, press tools, sheet metal components, and CNC & VMC machining work. We have been serving the manufacturing sector since 1991 and have built a reputation for delivering high-quality products and services to our clients. Our team of skilled engineers and technicians has extensive experience in the manufacturing industry and is committed to providing our clients with the best possible solutions for their manufacturing needs. We are dedicated to delivering outstanding results and exceeding our clients' expectations. We take pride in our work and are committed to providing our clients with the highest level of service and quality. We are always looking for new opportunities to grow and expand our business, and we are excited to work with you on your next project.

Our Products

Pipe Bending Tools

We offer a variety of pipe bending die & tool options, including custom-designed dies that are tailored to your specific requirements. Our team of skilled engineers and technicians has extensive experience in the making them run your production lines.

SPMs(Special Purpose Machines)

We specialize in the production of high-quality Special Purpose Machines (SPMs). Our SPMs are designed to reduce manufacturing time, increase efficiency, and improve product quality.

Jigs & Fixtures

We are a manufacturer of high-quality jigs and fixtures designed to improve manufacturing efficiency and precision. Our jigs and fixtures are custom-made to meet the unique needs of each client and can be used in a variety of manufacturing applications, from small-scale production to large-scale industrial manufacturing.

Guages

We are the reknowned manufacturer of high-quality gauges that are designed to meet the unique needs of each client. Our gauges are custom-made to meet the unique needs of each client and can be used in a variety of manufacturing applications, from small-scale production to large-scale industrial manufacturing.

Sheet Metal Components

We are a leading manufacturer of high-quality press tools and sheet metal components that are designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our press tools and sheet metal components are custom-made to provide the highest level of precision and quality.

CNC & VMC Machining

we have the expertise and resources to deliver outstanding results. We also offer press tools and sheet metal components, gauges, and machining work using state-of-the-art VMC and CNC technologies.
